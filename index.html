Deep Dive

I started working in the logistics industry at the age of 16. I was fascinated by supply chain issues, how globalization works and how it will affect our future lives. After graduating from high school with a degree in logistics, I decided to deepen my knowledge in this field and completed an apprenticeship in industrial engineering and international logistics. For me it is exciting to design, control and optimize processes economically. I learned how to strive for energy-efficient processes by analyzing and continuously improving them to increase efficiency and minimize costs. My thesis dealt with a management concept for a food tech company based in Switzerland with locations all over Europe. I am currently working as a software developer for a luxury retail company. The integration of informatics and data science into a company is necessary for its continued existence. This is the reason why I first pursued self-study and then decided to study Data Science on a part-time basis. I am looking forward to a great future with technologically advanced companies around the world.
